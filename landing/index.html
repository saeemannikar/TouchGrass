What it does

Everything Blender does.
With your bare hands.

🖐

Hand Tracking

MediaPipe reads 21 landmarks per hand at 20fps. An LSTM classifier maps gesture sequences to sculpt intents — not just positions.

🧱

Real Clay Physics

Voxel-based sculpting with Marching Cubes surface extraction. Volume preservation redistributes displaced clay like the real thing.

⚡

Native Performance

Rust voxel engine via Tauri. Marching Cubes runs 10–20x faster than browser JS. Inference stays in a Web Worker. 60fps renders.

🎯

Train Your Gestures

The in-app LSTM trainer learns your specific hand shape and movement style. 8 gestures, ~8 samples each, trains in 30 seconds.

💾

Export Anywhere

OBJ for general 3D use. Binary STL for 3D printing — slice straight into Bambu, Prusa, or Cura. Lossless format, small files.

🔓

Free. Forever.

MIT licensed. No account, no subscription, no telemetry, no ads. It's yours. Fork it, ship it, sell sculptures made with it.

Workflow

From download to sculpting in 3 steps.

01

Download & open

~8MB installer. No runtime, no dependencies. Opens in under 2 seconds. A sphere of clay is waiting for you.

02

Enable hands (optional: train)

Click ✋ Enable Hands, grant camera access. Works immediately with rule-based gestures. Run the in-app trainer once to calibrate to your hands for better accuracy.

03

Sculpt

Open palm pushes clay. Pinch pulls. Fist smooths. Spread fingers inflate. Two-hand pinch zooms. Pinch in empty space orbits. Mouse always works as fallback.

04

Export

Hit Export STL, open in your slicer, print. Or OBJ into Blender for further work. Ctrl+Z undoes any stroke, up to 64 steps.
